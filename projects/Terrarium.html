Terrarium

This Terrarium is a canvas of 10 little colorful worms, which sense the food in vicinity and try to get there first to eat. As they eat they grow. If there is no food vicinity, they get restless and randomly go around. Click anywhere on the canvas to toggle the visibility of food. Try full screen to view better. Size of the canvas depends on the width and height of the browers window at time of opening.
